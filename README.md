# WP2
WP2 sits at the start of the BMD value-chain and thus aims to develop a catalogue of resources of relevant biodiversity and environmental data, facilitate the mobilisation of historical baseline and legacy biodiversity datasets to FAIR data repositories, and connect Plug-n-Play high-throughput methods with AI taxon identification technologies. 

The aim is support better monitoring through high-throughput methods (image, sound, and environmental DNA), the mobilisation of high-throughput data and stakeholder datasets into relevant FAIR data repositories, and to build a catalogue of relevant data (biotic, environmental, climatic, remote sensing), including required standardised metadata, associated licensing and reuse information.

# Task 2.1 
Task 2.1 aims at identifying available biodiversity and environmental data resources relevant for the VREs (WP5), and building a queryable catalogue of these datasets augmented with metadata needed for spatio-temporal harmonisation in WP3. Identification will be facilitated by project partners’ direct involvement in data generation efforts and their connections to other relevant initiatives, as well as through stakeholder engagement activities of WP1 ensuring relevance for the VREs. Building the BMD data catalogue will leverage the power of the open source GeoNetwork informatics solutions for collating and managing spatially referenced data resources. 

The scripts uploaded to this repository belong to subtask 2.1.2 - Design and deployment of the GeoNetwork-based BMD Data Catalogue. Scripts will deliver technical solutions to build the queryable catalogue of identified datasets that support project needs. 
This repository evolved over time. At the beginning dedicated to wekeo only, now used for other datasources. Each datasource should be isolated in its own directory.
